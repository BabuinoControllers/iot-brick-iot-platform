# IoT Brick – Modular IoT Platform for Sensors and Dataloggers

IoT Brick is a secure, modular IoT device and platform designed to **connect sensors and dataloggers** to digital applications. The starter kit provides tools, examples, and documentation to quickly develop IoT solutions **without firmware development**, allowing developers to focus on the application logic itself.

---

## Key Features

- Modular IoT architecture for rapid prototyping  
- Functional block-based programming, no firmware coding required  
- Secure device communication  
- Easy integration with a wide range of sensors and dataloggers  
- Edge computing capabilities for real-time data processing  
- Accelerates time to market for IoT applications  

---

## Typical Applications

IoT Brick can be used in multiple IoT scenarios, including:

- Industrial monitoring and predictive maintenance  
- Structural health monitoring for buildings and infrastructure  
- Environmental sensing (air, water, temperature, vibration)  
- Smart infrastructure and IoT-enabled facilities  
- Data acquisition from sensors and dataloggers  

---

## Architecture


Sensors → IoT Brick → IoT Platform → Applications / Analytics / ERP

- **Sensors**: energy meeters, environmental sensors, , any other sensor.  
- **IoT Brick**: modular device managing inputs/outputs via configurable functional blocks  
- **IoT Platform**: cloud or on-premises platform for data ingestion, storage, and analysis  
- **Applications**: dashboards, analytics, machine learning, ERP integration

## Supported Sensors

IoT Brick can interface with a wide variety of sensors, including:

- **Energy & Power Monitoring**
  - Energy meters
  - Current sensors
  - Voltage sensors
  - Power quality monitors

- **Environmental Monitoring**
  - Temperature sensors
  - Humidity sensors
  - Air quality sensors (CO₂, VOCs, PM2.5/PM10)
  - Pressure sensors
  - Light / luminance sensors

- **Agriculture & Soil**
  - Soil moisture sensors
  - Soil pH sensors
  - Soil temperature sensors
  - Light / PAR sensors
  - Weather sensors (rain, wind, solar radiation)

- **Water & Liquid**
  - Water flow sensors
  - Water level sensors
  - Water quality sensors (pH, conductivity, turbidity)
  - Temperature sensors for liquids

- **Industrial & Factory**
  - Proximity sensors
  - Load cells / weight sensors
  - Gas sensors (methane, CO, hydrogen)
  - RFID / NFC readers

- **Smart Building / IoT**
  - Motion / occupancy sensors
  - Door/window contact sensors
  - Smoke / fire sensors
  - Sound / noise level sensors
  - Smart plugs / energy usage monitors
 
 - **Structural & Vibration**
  - Accelerometers
  - Gyroscopes
  - Strain gauges
  - Displacement sensors
  - Tilt sensors
  - Vibration sensors
